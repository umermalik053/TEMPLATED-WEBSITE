THE BEGINNING

After picking up the best stylists in town, we opened the doors to our elegant and posh men’s salon in April of 1990. Located in DHA, Gulshan-E-Iqbal, and Gulistan-E-Johar,North Karachi Malik Men’s Salon strives to deliver these areas glamour flared with an all-around city sensation.

The Malik Men’s Saloon staff’s classic vision is held chief to Karachi standards and is made up of an eclectic company of trend-setting artists from around Pakistan who all contribute to a passion for timeless and contemporary beauty and grace.
